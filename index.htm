Safe and reliable project

BNBFarm runs automatically on the blockchain and its smart contract is uploaded to the BNB blockchain. No one is able to edit or delete the smart contract, nor influence its autonomous operation. The dividends are also automatically paid through the smart contract.

Dividend distribute

BNBFarm smart contract generates 5-20% dividends per day based on your investment and distribute your dividends to your balance. For example, if you invest in the 15% plan, then you will get over 100% of your first deposit in 10 days. The dividends generate every second and you can withdraw or reinvest your dividends each second. When you reinvest, the total investment would increase and you will get more dividends.

Referral program

BNBFarm smart contract set 3 tiers of referral rewards, which are 4%, 2%, and 2% respectively.

How to invest on desktop?

You can install the MetaMask extension on Chrome. Go to settings.
CHANGE NETWORKS TO: Custom RPC
Network Name: Smart Chain
New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
Chain ID: 56
Currency Symbol (optional): BNB
Block Explorer URL (optional): https://bscscan.com
Then transfer the BNB from the exchange https://binance.com to the wallet. Finally, login on Metamask to browse this website and invest.

How to invest on mobile?

You can download Trust Wallet app from application store. After the installation, you can create a new BNB wallet or import an existing BNB wallet, and then transfer the BNB from the exchange to the wallet. Finally, find BNBFarm within the wallet app or browse our site with the browser in the app, and then go to invest or withdraw.
